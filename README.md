These data files support figures in our manuscript : Hobaiter C, Dominy N 2025 Firelight and the origins of spoken language

Quantifying firelight with a SpectraScan PR-670 spectroradiometer (Photo Research, Syracuse, USA) at a distance of 1 m and height of 75 cm. We provide the raw data for flames and coals, as well as the calculated data for luminance.
